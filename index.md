## 🚀 How to Use the Software

Launch the application using the desktop shortcut. You will see a workspace designed for photo selection and adjustment.

### Loading Images
Drag your image files directly into the main window. You can also click the "Browse Files" button to select photos from your folders. The software supports standard formats like JPEG, PNG, and TIFF.

### Applying Enhancements
The AI tool analyzes each photo upon import. It identifies issues like noise, blur, or low pixel density.

* Denoise: Toggle this feature to remove grain from photos taken in low light. The AI smooths out textures while keeping edges sharp.
* Sharpen: Correct motion blur or focus errors. Slide the strength bar to adjust the level of detail.
* Upscale: Increase the size of your images without loss of quality. You can scale photos up to 6 times their original size.

### Batch Processing
Process many photos at once to improve your workflow:

1. Select all the images in your list.
2. Adjust the global settings in the right-hand panel.
3. Click "Apply to All" to ensure every photo uses the same settings.
4. Choose an output folder where the software will save your new files.
5. Click "Start Batch" to begin the enhancement process.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Tips

* The software runs slow: Close other memory-intensive applications when processing large batches.
* The installer fails: Right-click the installer and choose "Run as Administrator."
* Changes do not save: Check that you have write access to your chosen output folder.
* Graphics issues: Ensure your graphics card drivers receive regular updates from the manufacturer.
